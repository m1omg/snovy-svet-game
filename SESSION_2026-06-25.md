## Technical gotchas (worth remembering)
- **Shared geometry:** every `THREE.Sprite` shares one global geometry, and we also share one star geometry; `disposeObject` must skip both (`!o.isSprite && o.geometry !== _starGeo`) or other copies vanish.
- **Headless verification:** Firefox `--screenshot` does **not** capture WebGL canvas pixels. Render offscreen with a `new THREE.WebGLRenderer({preserveDrawingBuffer:true})` → `toDataURL()` → put in an `<img>` (which it *does* capture). DOM overlays screenshot fine directly.
- **`pgrep -f` / `pkill -f` self-match** the Bash wrapper (the pattern is in your own command line) → can kill the shell. Use `fuser -k PORT/tcp` for ports, or split the literal.
- **Foreground `sleep` is blocked** by the Bash tool; pace polling with real network round-trips (`gh api`, `curl`) instead.
